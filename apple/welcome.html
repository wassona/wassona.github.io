Integrating Games and Activities Into Your Day

Everyone likes something new and fun. New fun physical activity ideas have the extra bonus of encouraging imagination, building strength and endurance, and supporting emotional health and general well-being.

Use Manzanitas Family Child Care's outdoor play area to get some new ideas for structured physical activity. Take your mouse and hover over the various elements and then click to get more information about each fun physical activity depicted in the scene.

Use the Healthy Apple Physical Activity Toolkit to find more ideas.
